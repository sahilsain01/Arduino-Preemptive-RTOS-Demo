DHT11 Sensor:
  VCC  → 5V (Arduino)
  GND  → GND (Arduino)
  DATA → Pin 2 (Arduino)

HC-SR04 Ultrasonic Sensor:
  VCC  → 5V (Arduino)
  GND  → GND (Arduino)
  TRIG → Pin 3 (Arduino)
  ECHO → Pin 4 (Arduino)

LED 1 (Ultrasonic Task):
  Anode (+) → Pin 9 (Arduino)
  Cathode (-) → 220Ω resistor → GND

LED 2 (Blink Task):
  Anode (+) → Pin 10 (Arduino)
  Cathode (-) → 220Ω resistor → GND

Built-in LED (Pin 13) used for DHT11 task indicator
